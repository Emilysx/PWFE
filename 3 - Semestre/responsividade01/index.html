Kuromi ~

Kuromi (クロミ, Kuromi) is a rabbit character from the Japanese company Sanrio, and part of the My Melody universe. She is the self-proclaimed doppelgänger rival of My Melody, and, while she may look tough, she is actually very girly. Kuromi's charm points are her black hood and her pink skull, she loves handsome guys and writing in her diary.
